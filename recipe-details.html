Quinoa Power Bowl

← Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas
  • 1/2 avocado
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Lemon dressing

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package.
  2. Assemble bowl with all ingredients.
  3. Drizzle with dressing and serve.

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 430 kcal

Protein: 18 g

Carbs: 52 g

Fat: 14 g
